JES was founded in 2014 to provide education, computer setup, and custom request for businesses and individuals. Currently located in Waco TX, we service personal and business setups in Waco, Austin, San Antonio, and Dallas. The great thing about JES is that we can not only be contacted by phone, but also via Skype, Google Hangouts, and FaceTime. Communication is key to us so if you need help from home we can log on and walk you through. JES is able to be your translator when it comes to communicating with your ISP(internet service provider). We also contact manufacturers regarding products that you may need help with and we offer on-call times when you need us the most for your personal and business computer needs. With the advent of VOIP (Voice over IP) and the work at home craze sweeping the world, you need someone who is up to date with the latest internet protocols and networking terminology. Hackers target networks that are rooted in poor setup and viruses are written every day to exploit these holes in security. At JES, we have two computers setup at all times and they have one job, to get infected. From the data collected from these computers, we can gain knowledge on how the viruses attack in order to better protect you. How will you know how to combat your attacker when you don't know his/her moves? JES has you covered from all angles.
